That moment chane my future later on when i was traveling to find the best place where I could sattle down in order so I can make music.

    • In 2003 I had my first band LOUD n´CLEAR

    it was a hard rock band influenced by the 80´s glam metal.
    With luck and a lot of hard work we managed to play in big metal festivals, win number 1 in a bands contest and

    The band decided to go on separate ways in 2005 but came back to record the album in 2012
    which called "Playing with Thunder and went on a few gigs.
    The album awas sold out in Germany but got more success in Sweden.

    After along decision, I decided that my music future should be in Stockholm and not continue in Israel.
    It has been almost 8 years since I arrived to Sweden, The start wasn´t easy but I played with several bands,
    several artists and now I am working on my own solo project.
    I really love Sweden, it can goes at winter time to -22 while it´s very dark and at summer time the temperature can goes to 25 degrees
