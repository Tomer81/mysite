My Rock n´Roll Journey

I was born in Israel and raised in the city Haifa, I didn´t listen to rock music until I found the band Queen which gave me a whole new perspective on music.
That moment chane my future later on when i was traveling to find the best place to sattle to make music.

some of the places I traveled:

  • London
  • Berlin
  • Helsinki

    • In 2003 I had my first band LOUD n´CLEAR

    it was a hard rock band influenced by the 80´s glam metal.
    With luck and a lot of hard work we managed to play in big metal festivals, win number 1 in a bands contest
    and open for Paul Dianno - Iron Maiden singer and Ron Bambelfoot who played guitar with Guns n´Roses at that time.
    The band decided to go on separate ways in 2005 but came back to record the album in 2012 which called "Playing with Thunder and went on a few gigs.
    The album awas sold out in Germany but got more success in Sweden.

    After along decision, I decided that my music future should be in Stockholm and not continue in Israel.
    It has been almost 8 years since I arrived to Sweden, The start wasn´t easy but I played with several bands,
    several artists and now I am working on my own solo project.
    I really love Sweden, it can goes at winter time to -22 while it´s very dark and at summer time the temperature can goes to 25 degrees
    and there is sun the whole day and night.
